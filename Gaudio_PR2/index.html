FLOWER SPARK

Join the fun at Washington’s family spring festival
May 5 & 6, 2017
National Cathedral, Washington, DC
JOIN ON FACEBOOK

ADMISSION IS FREE

Flower Mart reaches a wide cross section of the Washington region. On a nice spring day, Flower Mart daily attendance is over 10,000 people. Television, newsprint, radio stations and national publications give Flower Mart excellent coverage and local TV celebrities and weathermen broadcast from the Mart. The NW Current publishes our Program as a special Flower Mart edition, available in late April, for DC home delivery and at selected locations in Maryland.

DATES & HOURS:

FRIDAY MAY 5th, 10 AM TO 6 PM  & SATURDAY MAY 6th, 10 AM to 5 PM

VISITOR INFORMATION

Held on the grounds of Washington National Cathedral, Wisconsin & Massachusetts Aves., NW., Washington, DC. During the Mart, there is no parking on the streets of the Cathedral, however there is underground parking in the Cathedral garage. Any #30 Metro bus takes you to the Cathedral.

All proceeds from this event benefit the gardens and grounds of the National Cathedral
