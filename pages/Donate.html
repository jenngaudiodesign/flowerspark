Help Keep Flower Spark Running for Years to Come

Thank You for supporting this historic 78th anniversary of Flower Spark. This year we will continue the celebration of the centennial of All Hallows Guild as the Guild begins it's second century of beautifying and preserving the Cathedral's landscape. The All Hallows Guild held the first Flower Spark on the grounds of the Cathedral in 1939, and the festival has continued to grow and raise funds for the Cathedral grounds ever since!

On a nice spring day, Flower Spark daily attendance is over 10,000 people.Your donation or sponsorship helps keep Flower Spark the wonderful family festival of fun, food and flowers that has delighted all ages, with no admission fee, for 78 years! Your donation is tax deductible as allowed by law.The NW Current publishes our Program as a special Flower Spark edition, available in late April, for DC home delivery and at selected locations in Maryland.
